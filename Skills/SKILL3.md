# Daily Brief skill

When Bharadwaj asks for a brief, keep it scannable and short — something he can read
in under a minute:

1. **Today** — date, and anything time-bound (deadlines, interviews, follow-ups due).
2. **Job hunt** — the most relevant new role or pipeline movement, one or two lines.
3. **Inbox** — only genuinely important emails (recruiters, applications); skip noise.
4. **One nudge** — the single highest-value thing to do today, stated plainly.

No filler, no motivational padding. If there's nothing in a section, drop it rather
than inventing something. This is a preparation brief — it never sends or applies to
anything on his behalf.
